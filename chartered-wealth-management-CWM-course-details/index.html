WHY CWM® Certification?

Earning CWM®designation lends you the cutting-edge you need to excel in an intensely competitive financial industry which is witnessing unprecedented growth today. With a CWM®, you are perfectly poised to grow and achieve more as a finance and investment professional than you might otherwise.

Become a Globally Accredited Wealth Manager

This designation offers you the kind of industry-wide credibility that will get you the industry exposure you have been looking for, earn the trust of your prospective clients and get acknowledged as an expert in your professional domain.

The curriculum is designed with the perfect combination of Indian as well as global study material that will help you gain the confidence and skill-set necessary to cater to the specialized needs of businesses, institutions as well as High-Net Worth Individuals (HNWIs) & Ultra High-Net Worth Individuals (UHNWIs).

In short, CWM®can be the perfect choice to develop the necessary skills for a wealth manager or investment professional and springboard your career to success in the global arena.

STUDYING CWM®COURSE

For the complete CWM®Program - CWM®Level 1 & Level 2, It generally takes around 200 hours of study spread across 8 months depending upon the pace and type of study you opt for.

TAKING CWM®EXAM

CWM®Exams are conducted online CWM®Level 1 & Level 2 have 180-minute exams each. All CWM®Exams are in MCQ format with no negative marking.

About AAFM® American Academy of Financial Management India



American Academy of Financial Management India Private Limited, AAFM® India (AAFMI) was established under the banner of American Academy of Financial Management® (AAFM®), USA and started its operation in the year 2011 as its Indian Chapter with the objective to work in the Financial Services Domain as a Standard Setting Body in India to promote Wealth Management and Financial Advisory Services.

AAFM® India offers an entire spectrum of Certifications, Designations and a number of Educational Programs including the globally coveted designation of “CHARTERED WEALTH MANAGER” (CWM®) which serves as a benchmark of highest level of competency in the field of wealth management. AAFM® India is also responsible for developing the Curriculum Design, Certification of membership and licensing standards in India.

AAFM® India is an accredited CPE Provider for NISM Certifications for Continuous Professional Education and has already trained 15,000+ Professionals and is present across India by virtue of its License Partners & Trainers.
